Pits of Despair





CURRENTLY IN DEVELOPMENT AND CONSIDERED PRE-ALPHA. Welcome to the Pits of Despair mod for BG2:EE. What is it? Pits of Despair brings a modified version of Black Pits 2 into the main campaign of BG2:EE. It alters the story of Black Pits 2 and modifies it to fit within the structure of BG2:EE with both old and new content for a more satisfying experience. It includes: A new NPC Voghlin NPC New Items New Spells New Areas New Music Improved Sound Voice Acting
